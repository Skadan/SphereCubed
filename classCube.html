Cube class constructor.

The Cube execution begins here. Creates components to load and render a Cube. Automatically called by the Level during load.

private

Cube class destructor.

The Cube execution ends here. Required to free the Cube allocated memory. Automatically called when Level unload.

Make sure to unload all Cube resources.
